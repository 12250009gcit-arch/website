Rules & Regulations

The foundation for a productive, safe, and respectful workplace.

Essential Company Policies Handbook (2025)

Non-Discrimination Policy

The Company is committed to providing a work environment free of discrimination and harassment. All employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and dignity, regardless of background or status.

  • Zero tolerance for harassment or bullying.
  • Prompt and confidential investigation of all reported conduct violations.

Attendance and Punctuality

Reliable attendance is essential for operations. Employees must adhere to their scheduled working hours and communicate any absence or delay to their manager immediately.

  • Scheduled working hours: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
  • Formal request required for any planned time off (minimum 2 weeks' notice).
üîí Information Security & Privacy

Data Handling and Confidentiality

All sensitive company and client information must be handled according to strict confidentiality protocols. Employees are prohibited from sharing proprietary information outside of the designated need-to-know scope.

  • Do not use personal email for company business.
  • Always use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.

Device Usage

Company-issued devices must be used primarily for work purposes and adhere to all IT security settings.

  • Personal software installations are strictly prohibited without IT approval.
  • Do not leave devices unattended in public areas.
üí∞ Financial & Expense Reporting

Travel and Expense Policy

Expenses must be reasonable, necessary, and documented with valid receipts. All submissions must comply with the quarterly deadline (see calendar).

  • Receipts required for all items over \$25.
  • Air travel should be booked economy class unless explicitly approved by a department head.

Conflict of Interest

Employees must avoid any personal or financial activity that could conflict with the company's best interests. Disclose potential conflicts to HR immediately.
